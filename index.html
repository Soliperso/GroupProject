Nobel Prize Winners

How to use this app

The Nobel Prize is one of the most distinguished endowments a person could be given. Laureates represent some of the most astute minds in the world, and their unrivaled contributions to society are a trademark of their tenacity and steadfastness.

First awareded in 1901, with nearly 1000 recipients spanning 75 different countries, one could speculate contributions have been nearly as diverse as the number of ethnic origins. The true value this app gleams by facilitating the ability to search for laureates by way of: country of origin, year the prize was awarded, or field of study.
